Planting the Invitation

Your invitation is printed on wildflower seed paper! To grow the seeds, follow these instructions:

Instructions

  • 1. Plant under a thin layer (1/8") of well-composted soil in a sunny location.
  • 2. Moisten the seed paper completely, and keep it moist until seeds germinate (~5-12 days).
  • 3. Water as needed, and be careful not to overwater seedlings.
  • 4. Enjoy your wildflowers and leave some for the pollinators!
